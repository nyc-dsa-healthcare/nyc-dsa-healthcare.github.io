Welcome to the NYC DSA Healthcare Working Group website! We're organizing for healthcare justice and Medicare for All.

## Our Mission

The Healthcare Working Group is dedicated to:

- Fighting for single-payer healthcare
- Organizing workers in the healthcare industry
- Building solidarity across the healthcare system
- Educating the public about healthcare justice
